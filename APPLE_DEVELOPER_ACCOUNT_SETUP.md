# How to Set Up Apple Developer Account

This guide will walk you through creating and setting up an Apple Developer account, which is required for Apple Sign-In.

---

## 📋 Prerequisites

Before you start, you need:

1. ✅ **Apple ID** (free Apple account - if you don't have one, create it first)
2. ✅ **Credit Card** (for payment)
3. ✅ **Valid Government ID** (for identity verification)
4. ✅ **Legal Entity Information** (company name, address, etc. if registering as an organization)

---

## 💰 Cost

- **Individual Developer**: $99 USD per year
- **Organization Developer**: $99 USD per year
- **Enterprise Developer**: $299 USD per year (for large companies)

**Note**: Payment is annual and automatically renews unless cancelled.

---

## 🚀 Step-by-Step Enrollment

### Step 1: Create or Sign In with Apple ID

1. Go to [Apple Developer Portal](https://developer.apple.com/)
2. If you don't have an Apple ID:
   - Click **"Create Apple ID"** or go to [appleid.apple.com](https://appleid.apple.com/)
   - Fill in your information (name, email, password, security questions)
   - Verify your email address
3. If you already have an Apple ID:
   - Click **"Account"** (top right)
   - Sign in with your Apple ID

---

### Step 2: Start Enrollment

1. After signing in, go to [developer.apple.com/programs](https://developer.apple.com/programs/)
2. Click **"Enroll"** button
3. You'll see two options:
   - **Individual**: For personal developers
   - **Organization**: For companies/organizations

**Choose the option that applies to you.**

---

### Step 3: Choose Account Type

#### Option A: Individual Developer

**Best for**: Personal projects, freelancers, solo developers

1. Select **"Individual"**
2. Click **"Start Your Enrollment"**
3. You'll need:
   - Your full name (must match your government ID)
   - Date of birth
   - Phone number
   - Address

#### Option B: Organization Developer

**Best for**: Companies, businesses, teams

1. Select **"Organization"**
2. Click **"Start Your Enrollment"**
3. You'll need:
   - Legal entity name (must match business registration)
   - D-U-N-S Number (9-digit identifier for your business)
   - Legal entity type (Corporation, LLC, Partnership, etc.)
   - Authorized representative information

**Note**: Getting a D-U-N-S Number can take 1-2 weeks. You can get it free from [dunsnumber.dnb.com](https://www.dnb.com/duns-number.html)

---

### Step 4: Complete Your Profile

Fill in all required information:

1. **Personal Information**:

   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Phone number
   - Mailing address

2. **Payment Information**:

   - Credit card or debit card
   - Billing address

3. **Agreements**:
   - Read and accept the **Apple Developer Program License Agreement**
   - Read and accept the **Paid Applications Agreement** (if applicable)

---

### Step 5: Identity Verification

Apple will verify your identity:

1. **For Individuals**:

   - You may be asked to verify your identity via phone or email
   - In some cases, you may need to provide government-issued ID

2. **For Organizations**:
   - Apple will verify your business entity
   - You may need to provide business registration documents
   - D-U-N-S Number verification

---

### Step 6: Payment

1. Enter your payment information:

   - Credit card or debit card
   - Cardholder name
   - Card number, expiry date, CVV
   - Billing address

2. Review the payment:

   - Amount: $99 USD per year
   - Auto-renewal: Enabled by default (you can cancel later)

3. Submit payment

---

### Step 7: Wait for Approval

1. **Processing Time**:

   - **Individual**: Usually approved within 24-48 hours
   - **Organization**: Can take 1-2 weeks (due to D-U-N-S verification)

2. **Email Notification**:

   - You'll receive an email when your enrollment is approved
   - Check your email regularly

3. **Status Check**:
   - You can check your enrollment status at [developer.apple.com/account](https://developer.apple.com/account)

---

### Step 8: Activate Your Account

Once approved:

1. You'll receive a confirmation email
2. Sign in to [developer.apple.com](https://developer.apple.com/)
3. Your account is now active! 🎉

---

## ✅ After Account Activation

Once your account is active, you can:

1. **Access Developer Tools**:

   - Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles
   - App Store Connect
   - Developer documentation

2. **Get Your Team ID**:

   - Go to [developer.apple.com/account](https://developer.apple.com/account)
   - Click on your account name (top right)
   - Your **Team ID** is displayed (you'll need this for Firebase setup)

3. **Create Identifiers and Keys**:
   - Create Service IDs for Sign In with Apple
   - Create Keys for authentication
   - Configure domains and return URLs

---

## 🔄 Account Renewal

- **Auto-Renewal**: Your account automatically renews each year
- **Payment**: Charged to the card on file
- **Cancellation**: You can cancel auto-renewal anytime (account remains active until expiry)
- **Expiry**: If payment fails, you have 30 days to update payment

## 💡 Tips and Best Practices

### 1. Use a Business Email

- Use a professional email address (not personal)
- This email will be associated with your developer account

### 2. Keep Documents Handy

- Have your government ID ready
- For organizations, have business registration documents ready

### 3. D-U-N-S Number (For Organizations)

- Apply early - it can take 1-2 weeks
- Free to obtain from [dunsnumber.dnb.com](https://www.dunsnumber.dnb.com/)
- Must match your business legal name exactly

### 4. Payment Method

- Use a credit card (not debit card if possible)
- Ensure sufficient funds available
- Keep payment method updated

### 5. Account Security

- Enable two-factor authentication
- Use a strong password
- Keep your Apple ID secure

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Issue: Enrollment Pending

- **Solution**: Wait 24-48 hours for processing. Check email for updates.

### Issue: Payment Failed

- **Solution**:
  - Check card has sufficient funds
  - Verify card details are correct
  - Try a different payment method

### Issue: D-U-N-S Number Not Found

- **Solution**:
  - Verify your business name matches exactly
  - Wait for D-U-N-S Number to be registered in Apple's system
  - Contact Apple Developer Support if needed

### Issue: Identity Verification Failed

- **Solution**:
  - Ensure information matches your government ID
  - Contact Apple Developer Support for assistance
